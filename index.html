Introduction

As the consumption of drugs among students increases year on year and it comes in new forms which are difficult to identify as drugs.The students are trapped by drug mafia at the school premises itself and later they are abused by them.At the same time Government,school authorities ,NGOs are relentlessly running awareness campaigns,deaddiction programmes among students which is not hitting the target audience unfortunately.The effort,time,money put into anti drug campaign activities by govt and non-govt organizations are result in almost null outcome.

View All

The District panchayath of Kasaragod was worried about the situations and they want to take effective measures or steps against drug consumption among students and they asked Kerala Startup mission and Central university of Kerala to prepare detailed study on the root causes of increasing consumption of drugs among students.Also they want to explore the possibilities of resisting this with the use of technology.

The Public Administration department of CUK discussed this and decided to conduct a survey among 15 different schools at different geographical locations,different category such as government,aided,private.Approx 1500 students,500 teachers ,500 parents were part of the survey and they arrived at some conclusions and interesting finding during survey analysis which is published in this website.

Now on the basis of analysis we are seeking for Technology solutions which can bring down the number of students inclining towards drug consumption and engaging students itself in anti drug campaign in an interesting manner.Details of requirement also published in this website as problem statement.

Problem Statement

Technology solution to report drug usage or deals and create awareness

♦ A technology solution to report consumption and dealing of drugs near campuses by keeping the anonymity of the reporting person.

♦Address the issue of rare chances of a technology solution being used just to report drug consumption. There should be some engaging contents or rewards or recognition, encouraging the user to adopt the solution.

♦A Solution which can communicate all relevant information including important such Contact details ,Department details whichever you feel relevant.

♦This should be interactive in such a way that enables discussion/queries related to drug abuse, keeping the identity of the participants of the discussion anonymous.

♦Facilitate sharing interesting contents in social media.

♦Rating or honoring by giving badge to those who are posting relevant contents at frequent intervals against drug usage

♦Engage NGOs/institutions so that they can run campaign, competitions against drug usage.

♦A platform for identifying the behavioral changes after the consumption through emotion recognition

♦One or a combination of the above mentioned points or any other technological solutions which can be effectively deployed to address the issue of drug abuse.
